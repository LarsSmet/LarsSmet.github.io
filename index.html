Merkhet's Decay

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur.

In this game, players step into the shoes of Merkhet, a half-god blessed with the power to manipulate time. However, here's the intriguing twist: Merkhet begins the game already infected. Can you save Egypt while battling against the encroaching infection?

I made this project with one of my closest friends. My primary focus was on designing and prototyping, whereas my friend took charge of the level blockout and delved into learning Unity lighting techniques. Our project aims to breathe new life into the popular zombie genre by infusing it with innovative elements. We achieved this by putting the game in an Ancient Egyptian setting and introducing unique abilities never before explored within the genre.

Extendo-Brawl

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur.

Always dreamed of smashing boxes at your friends? No worries, Extendo-Brawl has got you covered. In this game, players use an extendable arm to grapple around the level and throw boxes at each other. The last player standing wins the game.

This was the first group project I ever did for the course Game Projects. It was a very fun experience to work together on a long term project. Along the way, I had the pleasure of forging new friendships, and despite facing some challenges, we took pride in the final outcome.

For more info and devlogs, make sure to check out our itch.io page!

Spider VS ants

Play as a deadly spider on a mission to infiltrate and destroy the ant nest in 'Spider vs Ants'. Navigate through a maze of treacherous tunnels, overcome ant hordes and find the queen and her eggs. Your mission is clear: eradicate the queen and ensure the destruction of her future ant soldiers.

This was the first time that I worked in Unity and I instantly fell in love with the engine. I had a really fun time working on this project and it really sparked my interest for game design and prototyping.
